Welcome!

Welcome to the Harrisonburg Foodie Finder Website! Our goal is to provide locals, students, and visitors with information about the city's best places to eat or grab a drink! Harrisonburg's amazing variety of locally-owned restaurants, breweries, and wineries will sure leave a lasting impression. Of course however, if you would like to go back to the basics, Harrisonburg also presents its fair share of chain restaurants to crush that classic craving.

About Us

The Harrisonburg Foodie Finder website started with the goal of connecting locals and visitors to some of Harriosnburg's best resaurants, breweries, coffee shops, dessert spots, etc.! Being located right off of Highway 81, a twenty-minute drive from the famous Massanutten resort, and serving students from James Madison University, the town of Harrisonburg is constantly growing and welcoming new businesses.
