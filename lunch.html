Local Lunch Spots

Known for its many farm-to-table restaurants, Harrisonburg prides itself in providing fresh, local ingredients. Many restaurants in Harrisonburg work with local farms to serve unique and one-of-a-kind menu items. Whether you are craving a juicy burger or a fruity açaí bowl we have you covered!

Keep scrolling to discover some of Harrisonburg's finest local lunch places!

1. Magpie Diner

What started as a tire service station in the 1950s, Magpie's has now blossomed into a Harrisonburg staple. This popular Harrisonburg location is known for both its amazing weekend brunch food and freshly brewed coffee from Chestnut Ridge Coffee Roasters right next door. This local spot is definitely a place to check out, but make sure you plan ahead, it's a local favorite!

2. Rocktown Kitchen

As a restaurant that prides itself in its farm-to-table food, Rocktown Kicthen is the plce to be if you are looking for a more upscale, artisan-driven experience. Enjoy their locally-sourced, seasonal American cuisine while enjoying a drink either inside, at the bar, or outdoor patio. Rocktown Kitchen also provides spaces to host your next event!

3. Jack Brown's Beer & Burger Joint

Popular among the local college students, Jack Brown's Beer & Burger Joint is the perfect plce to go when you are looking for a nice, juicy burger. With it's locally-sourced beef, Jack Brown's provides the opportunity for its customers to create their own wacky burger creations with an abundance of toppings and a beer on the side.

4. The Little Grill

The Little Grill is exactly what it sounds like, little. This intimate, small restaurant offers amazing American cuisine items and to-die-for brunch entrées. Its amazing customer service makes your dining experience feel personal, so personal that wait times are sometimes three hours!

5. Clementine Cafe

Perhaps one of Harrisonburg's most popular spots, Clementine Cafe offers a plethera of services and events. Its amazing American cuisine can be enjoyed by a nice drink at the bar while enjoying live music both inside and outside. P.S. Don't forget to check this place out at night for a nice bar atmosphere!
